- structure:
  - `lane3_slowest_exh`
  - `lane3_worst_st`
- outer-shape refinement:
  - exactly one of `lane5` / `lane6` is `A-class`
  - here `A-class` means `A1` or `A2`
- ticket:
  - `3連単 1-2-4 BOX`
  - `6` tickets
  - `600円` per race under the current evaluation convention

## Working Mechanism

- lane `3` looks weak enough to remove from the `1..4` finish frame
- the remaining inside structure concentrates on `1,2,4`
- the branch improves when outer pressure is asymmetric:
  - one of `lane5` / `lane6` is `A-class`
  - but not both

This is the cleanest current interpretation:

- `lane3` weakness is the primary driver
- `lane5/lane6 one-A-only` is the main refinement
- `lane3_class` itself is not the core explanation
